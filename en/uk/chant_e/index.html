How to Chant e

This is a new writing system in which vowel-consonant sequences are written as units; each unit is based on a vowel letter, and consonant notation is secondary. It derived from a meme "chant e" in mcbbs.
Attention: this is a writing system, and it has its independent vocabulary, also for phonetic use.
